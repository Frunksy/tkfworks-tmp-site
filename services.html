Services

Workflow improvements

  • Map the current workflow (end-to-end) and identify bottlenecks, rework, and handoffs.
  • Design a simpler “standard way” with clear steps and ownership.
  • Build practical checklists, SOPs, and handover points.
  • Set up a simple operating rhythm (weekly priorities, metrics, actions).

Custom software development

  • Small internal tools to remove manual admin and double-handling.
  • Forms, trackers, dashboards, quoting helpers, and workflow automation.
  • Focus on maintainable, fit-for-purpose solutions (not overbuilt systems).

If software isn’t the right answer, we’ll tell you — often the biggest win is a cleaner process first.

Quoting and estimating (SMP)

  • Improve quoting consistency: scope, assumptions, exclusions, and change control.
  • Estimating templates/checklists to reduce missed items and margin leakage.
  • Better handover from quote → delivery (so what was sold matches what gets done).

Team leadership development

  • Leading safety and quality expectations by example.
  • Practical tools for team leads: planning the day/week, setting expectations, feedback, and follow-through.
  • Simple ways to run effective pre-starts and short toolbox meetings.
  • Build accountability without drama: roles, standards, and escalation paths.

Safety talks

  • Short, relevant sessions tailored to your work environment.
  • Clear takeaways: what to watch for, what “good” looks like, and what to do when something isn’t right.
  • Designed for engagement, not box-ticking.

Engagement options

  • Diagnostic: quick review with findings + prioritised plan.
  • Delivery: scoped project with clear outputs and a timeline.
  • Ongoing support: regular check-ins to keep improvements moving.
